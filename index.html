Music

Beyond the world of physics, I find creative expression through music. My musical journey began alongside my academic pursuits, earning a minor in Music from the University of Michigan. Music provides a different lens through which to explore patterns, harmonies, and the beauty of structured creativity.

Musical Journey

Music has been a constant companion throughout my academic and research career. Just as physics seeks to understand the fundamental patterns that govern our universe, music explores the patterns and relationships that create harmony and emotional resonance.

The analytical thinking required for physics research often informs my approach to composition and sound design, while the creative processes in music provide fresh perspectives for tackling complex research problems.
