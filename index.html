Research Focus

The MiniBooNE Anomaly

Investigating, experimentally and phenomenologically, the long-standing MiniBooNE excess of electro-magnetic events

MiniBooNE and MicroBooNE Combined Fit to a 3 + 1 Sterile Neutrino Scenario Search for an anomalous excess of charged-current quasielastic νe interactions with the MicroBooNE experiment using Deep-Learning-based reconstruction Explaining the MiniBooNE Excess Through a Mixed Model of Oscillation and Decay Implications of MicroBooNE’s low sensitivity to electron antineutrino interactions in the search for the MiniBooNE excess

Heavy Neutral Leptons

Searching for the well-motivated right-handed counterpoarts to our left-handed neutrinos

Constraints and sensitivities for dipole-portal heavy neutral leptons from ND280 and its upgrade Dipole-coupled heavy-neutral-lepton explanations of the MiniBooNE excess including constraints from MINERvA data

Ultra-High-Energy Neutrinos

On IceCube and KM3NeT, understanding what neutrino telescopes can tell us about the highest-energy fundamental particles we've ever observed.

Ultrahigh-Energy Event KM3-230213A within the Global Neutrino Landscape

Natural Neutrino Detectors

Exploring the question: can we leverage nature to explore Nature?

The Neutrino Kaleidoscope: Searches for Non-Standard Neutrino Oscillations at Neutrino Telescopes with a TeV Muon Accelerator Source Lake- and Surface-Based Detectors for Forward Neutrino Physics

Learning Efficient Representations of Neutrino Telescope Events Enhancing events in neutrino telescopes through deep-learning-driven superresolution Convolutional neural networks for shower energy prediction in liquid argon time projection chambers
